Frequently Asked Questions
Is StudyAI really free? ▾
Yes! StudyAI offers a generous free tier that includes AI chat, quiz generation, and flashcard creation. Premium features are available for power users who need unlimited access.
What AI model does StudyAI use? ▾
StudyAI is powered by Claude, one of the most capable AI assistants available. This means you get accurate, thoughtful, and helpful responses for any academic topic.
Can I upload my own study materials? ▾
Absolutely! You can upload PDFs, text documents, and images of handwritten notes. Our AI will analyze your materials and generate quizzes, flashcards, and summaries tailored to your content.
Which subjects are supported? ▾
StudyAI works with virtually any academic subject — from Computer Science and Mathematics to Biology, History, Literature, and more. If you can study it, StudyAI can help.
Is my data secure? ▾
Yes, we take your privacy seriously. All uploaded materials and conversation data are encrypted. We never share your personal information or study data with third parties.
